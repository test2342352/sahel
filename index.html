    ABOUT US

    Founded in 2008 with the names of Andalus Packed Cement Distribution Company and coverage of the northern part of the Republic, there was a vision and goal to become the largest supplier of cement in the northern region of the Republic, and then only two years later we became agents of Ameriya Cement Company in Alexandria and Matrouh.

    In 2016, we touched upon the distribution of bulk cement to concrete plants. The name of the company was changed from Andalusia to Sahel Company. This name was named because our company was one of the best in distributing cement in the entire northern region of the Republic, which is the coast of Egypt. In 2019, the company was contracted with the National Cement Plant in Beni Suef, which is the largest cement factory in the Middle East.
